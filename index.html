All About Pies

The Social Pie is an organic pie shop located in Ottawa's Byward Market. We offer over 180 delicious craft pie varieties and feature ten pie flavours in our store at any given day from our delicious Zucchini Pot Pie to our best-selling Wildberry Apple Pie.
